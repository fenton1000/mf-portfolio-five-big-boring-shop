About Us

Big Boring Shop is an online grocery store with a simple belief. Shopping can be fun..... but the weekly grocery shop simply isn't! So don't waste time going to the supermarket week after week after week.... Just order online and we will deliver to your door. To save even more time you can create an account, make a favourites list and then quickly add these to you cart. Easy!

So it must be expensive then, right?

NO!! Our clear aim is compete with similar low cost retailers. As a fully online store we do not carry the high cost of high street retail. Furthermore we keep our warehouses stocked with a well refined inventory of the most popular items. In this way we keep our system and offering simple and efficient, passing the savings on to you!

Delivery

First things first! The cost?

Free delivery throughout Ireland on all orders of €30 or more.

As a fully online store, delivery is kind of our thing! We have and extensive network of warehouses throughout the country and we operate our own fleet of delivery vehicles. As the vast majority of our customers use us for the big weekly shop delivery charges rarely arise.... Despite our incredible value you will likely spend the minimun of €30! For deliveries less of than €30 a €10 delivery fee applies.
